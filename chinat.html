Chinatown effects

Background: The China town effect aims to unveil contextual yet critical personal languange of how each individual perceive china town through architecture.The project objective is to investigate the notion of commonality and stereotype - in relationship to the world of Chinatown(s) as social typologies.

Target Audience: people with age between 16-60 years old that interested in art or architectural exhibition.

Role and Responsibilities: Carpet Design team , responsible for researching, designing the carpet to goes together with the rest of the pavillion along with making sure that the story was smoothly portrayed on the Carpets.

Emphathize

During research phase, we gathered data of what people think of or how they perceive china town in various way. In conclusion, we decided to use these 3 category that we thought are interesting to be develop further which are iconography / traditional symbol , architectural element and the used of fabric.

Audience feedbacks

Define and Ideate

To ensure the carpet matches the pavilion precisely, we must collaborate closely with the column team and the main structure team. This way, we can determine the final shape of the carpet to match the pavilion's outer shape

Mockup

In our initial prototype phase, we diligently pursued the optimal color palette and layout for each element, intending to effectively convey these features on the carpet. These elements, inspired by the sights of Chinatown, will be meticulously integrated into the pavilion's interior carpet design, imparting a sensation that every step taken within the pavilion mirrors a journey through these culturally significant elements.
