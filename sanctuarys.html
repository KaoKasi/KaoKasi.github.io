Sanctuary Serenity

Background: Our project's core aim is to reintegrate the concept of the temple into people's lives, all while recognizing the demands of today's digital-centric lifestyle that often leads to disconnection. We intend to rejuvenate mental well-being, serving as a catalyst for reconnection with one's inner self and fostering genuine human-to-human interactions in the physical world.The sanctuary position itself as platform that bring nirvana to people without related to religion.

Target Audience: People who open to exploring and experiencing a sense of inner peace and serenity, irrespective of religious affiliations.

Role and Responsibilities: Researching - Conducting Interview with locals - Mockup the model - Test the design - Design information graphic - Editing vdos - Design the architecture model

Emphathize
