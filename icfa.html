International Climate Finance Accelerator Luxembourg (ICFA)

Background: In collaboration with the International Climate Finance Accelerator Luxembourg (ICFA), I contributed to a project focused on promoting high-impact investment strategies. Our specific task was to create a video that educates individuals on the potential to generate income through clean energy initiatives and how they can sell this energy to the government. This project aimed to raise awareness about the financial opportunities associated with environmentally friendly practices while supporting ICFA's mission to advance climate finance solutions

Target Audience: This project targeted potential partners and investors interested in sustainable ventures and financial growth

Role and Responsibilities: created artwork and animations aligned with our established art direction to promote our initiative, emphasizing income opportunities in the clean energy sector, including government sales.
