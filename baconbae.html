Bacon Bae

Background: Bacon Bae is an NFT project in which I participated as a member of the social media team, focused on fostering engagement and building a vibrant community in preparation for its launch on OpenSea

Target Audience: To appeal to our international audience, we intentionally created Bacon Bae as a character that young Americans and people from around the world can relate to. Our plan is to regularly show Bacon Bae participating in typical American activities like surfing, shopping for groceries, and sunbathing

Role and Responsibilities: In charge of creating marketing posts for sharing on Twitter and Discord.
